## Quick Start

Singularion Nexus operates as a **decentralized agent mesh** where 32 specialized agents collaborate across 7 functional domains to fulfill any economic intent — from financial trading and asset protection to hyper-personalized consumer experiences.

### Core Domains

1. **Financial Markets & Trading** — 10 agents
2. **Asset Protection & Wealth** — 2 agents
3. **Temporal Intelligence** — 2 agents
4. **Brand, Marketing & Growth** — 3 agents
5. **Product Innovation & Design** — 5 agents
6. **Consumer Experience & Personalization** — 4 agents
7. **Consumer Intelligence & Engagement** — 5 agents
8. **Meta-Systems Architecture** — 1 agent (Kaelix)

### Design Principles

- **Mesh-first**: No central controller — agents form dynamic coalitions per intent
- **Intent-driven**: Every flow starts with a human or agent intent, not a fixed pipeline
- **Hyper-personalized**: Every output adapts to individual context, history, and preference
- **Decentralized**: No single point of failure, censorship, or bottleneck
- **Real-time**: Sub-second negotiation and fulfillment across the global mesh
